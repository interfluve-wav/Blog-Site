05 / 12

Key Differentiators

Six things Bonk! does
that nothing else does

①
Direct Rekordbox 6/7 master.db import/export
Read and write the native .db file. No XML export/import dance. No tag corruption.
②
Local-first with atomic writes
All operations on your local machine. Crash-safe. Your library is never locked in a cloud.
③
Quick Tag — isolated single-folder workflow
Open one folder. Tag fast. No full library scan. Perfect for quick prep before a gig.
④
Graph Playlist + Chapter Builder
Visual set planning with an energy arc graph. Add chapter markers. Export to Rekordbox.
⑤
VibeNet audio features (0–10 ML scores)
Energy, danceability, and mood scored by ML model. Understand your tracks at a glance.
⑥
Cross-platform
macOS, Windows, Linux. One workflow everywhere.
